# Unity Scenes and Lighting

Detect Built-in, URP, or HDRP. Never copy pipeline-specific settings blindly.

Review:
- focal hierarchy and navigation readability
- key, fill, rim, ambient, and emissive balance
- baked versus realtime lighting strategy
- light probes and reflection probes
- shadow distance and cascade cost
- fog and volume consistency
- exposure and tone mapping
- camera clipping, FOV, framing, and transitions
- gameplay readability under all intended times or weather states

Prefer a coherent lighting language over many uncoordinated lights.
