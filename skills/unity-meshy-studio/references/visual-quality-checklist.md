# Unity visual-quality checklist

## Composition
- Clear focal point
- Deliberate visual hierarchy
- Readable silhouettes
- Controlled density and negative space
- Consistent scale language

## UI
- Consistent grid, padding, typography, iconography, and corner treatment
- Hover, pressed, selected, disabled, loading, empty, success, warning, and error states
- Safe areas and responsive constraints
- Contrast and text-size accessibility
- No clipped panels or overlapping labels

## World
- Coherent lighting and color script
- Material consistency
- Fog and post-processing used intentionally
- Decals and small detail support gameplay readability
- LODs, occlusion, batching, and texture budgets reviewed

## Animation
- Strong anticipation and follow-through
- No obvious foot sliding
- Stable contact points
- No severe mesh clipping or collapsing joints
- Loop transitions are continuous
